📜 The Gauntlet Challenge: Official Rules

The Goal: Survive the Gauntlet with at least one deck remaining. You win the cash prize by defeating 10 Gauntlet decks in total across your 3 decks.

🃏 Deck Construction

  • Build 3 decks, each with a minimum of 60 cards and a 15-card sideboard.
  • You cannot have more than 4 copies of any single card across all your decks and sideboards combined (basic lands excluded).
  • All decks must be built using the legal card pool on this site.

💰 Cash Prize & Fallen Challengers

The prize pot grows by $10 after every fallen challenger.

⚡ Challenger's Advantages

  • You decide who plays first in the first game of every match.
  • You choose which of your decks to send into each battle.

🏆 Match Rules & The "Tag" Safety Net

  • Each battle against a Gauntlet deck is a best-two-out-of-three games match. After each game, players may use sideboards.
  • When a Gauntlet deck is defeated, it is removed from the Gauntlet.
  • First-Match Safety Net (The "Tag"): Applies only during your first match. If you lose the first game, you may use a one-time "Tag" to immediately swap to a different deck to finish the current match. If you lose this match after using your Tag, the Gauntlet Master will eliminate one of your three decks (any deck, even one not yet played).

💀 Losing the Gauntlet

You lose the Gauntlet when your final deck is eliminated.

  • Damage Spells: Can't deal more damage than their CMC
  • Kill Spells: Unconditional removal costs 5+ mana; conditional removal is legal at lower costs
  • Counterspells: Unconditional counters must cost 4+ mana
  • Board Wipes: Must cost 6+ mana
  • Land Destruction: Must cost 4+ mana
  • Mana Rocks: Under 3 CMC must enter tapped, can only produce 1 mana
  • Set Restrictions: Only cards from approved sets are legal
  • General: Cards that violate these guidelines are automatically banned by the system.
