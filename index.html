📜 The Gauntlet Challenge: Official Rules

The Goal: Survive the Gauntlet with at least one deck remaining. You win the cash prize by defeating 10 Gauntlet decks in total across your 3 decks.

🃏 Deck Construction

  • Build 3 decks, minimum 60 cards + 15-card sideboard
  • No more than 4 copies of any card across all decks

💰 Cash Prize

The prize pot grows by $10 after every fallen challenger.

⚡ Challenger's Advantages

  • You decide who plays first in the first game of every match.
  • You choose which of your decks to send into each battle.

🏆 Match Rules & The "Tag" Safety Net

  • Best-two-out-of-three games match. Sideboards allowed.
  • First-Match Safety Net (The "Tag"): One-time tag to swap decks if you lose first game.
  • Damage Spells: Can't deal more damage than CMC
  • Kill Spells: Unconditional removal costs 5+ mana
  • Counterspells: Unconditional counters cost 4+ mana
  • Board Wipes: Must cost 6+ mana
  • Land Destruction: Must cost 4+ mana
  • Mana Rocks: Under 3 CMC must enter tapped, only produce 1 mana
  • Set Restrictions: Only cards from approved sets are legal
